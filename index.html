Program of Work Agreement

SSOS v3.0 · Transition Intelligence Managed Service · $5,400 + GST

Copper Kettle Consulting — Spiderman SE Operating System v3.0 · April 2026

This Agreement is entered into between Copper Kettle Consulting Pty Ltd (ABN 45 679 481 040) ("CKC") and Spiderman SE Eco Pest Management (ABN 16 294 708 407) (Sarah Jane Cutting, "Client").


1. Scope & Timeline. CKC will deliver the Spiderman SE Operating System (SSOS) — a 27-project, 18-month transformation program structured across five phases as outlined in this portal and the SSOS Program Proposal v3.0 dated April 2026. Target delivery is 18 months, or less, from the 8 April 2026 commencement. The engagement is subject to a 24-month Maximum Delivery Term (Long-stop). This hard cap prevents duplication of efforts and ensures the transformation remains synchronised with the Client’s current business environment.


2. Delivery Model & AI Safety. This engagement is delivered under the Transition Intelligence Managed Service model.


2.1. AI Use Augmentation. All inputs and outputs are planned, structured, written, reviewed, edited, assessed and approved by Zara Grewal, your assigned Transition Intelligence Architect, prior to delivery. CKC maintains Professional Indemnity and Cyber Insurance covering AI-related risks and will not use Client data to train public AI models, ensuring compliance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Austrlian Privacy Principles or APPs).


3. Fees & Payment. Monthly retainer$5,400 + GST for ongoing delivery delivery of the Spiderman SE Program of Work until full completion as defined by SSOS Program of Work v3.0.


3.1. Seasonal Retainer Allowance. The retainer may be temporarily decreased/increased for periods if mutually agreed during 3-month reviews.


4. Payment Cadence. The monthly retainer may be paid monthly, as selected at checkout. First payment is due at Program of Work Agreement.


5. Program Checkpoints. 3 monthly formal reviews are built in. These are collaborative reviews, not exit points. The full delivery of the SSOS Program of Work v3.0 engagement stands — checkpoints ensure the program remains fit for purpose as the business evolves.


5.1. Dependencies. Timely delivery relies on Client providing necessary access/data. Delays caused by third-party platforms (e.g., GorillaDesk, Employment Hero) exceeding 7 days will be flagged as risks and subject to the necessary escalation protocol.


6. Project Prioritisation. A maximum of 6 projects will be active simultaneously. Projects are sequenced by effort-vs-impact. The initial priority order is: Project 9 (Employment Hero), Project 11 (SOPs/JSAs/SWMS), Project 23 (Call Management), Project 24 (Termite Forms).


7. Intellectual Property.. Intellectual Property. Client owns specific deliverables (SOPs, forms, integration configs) upon payment of the relevant monthly retainer. CKC retains rights to its underlying Transition Intelligence™ methodologies, frameworks, and AI toolsets.


8. Confidentiality. Both parties maintain confidentiality of all proprietary and sensitive information shared during this engagement.


9. Variation. Changes to scope or phasing are agreed in writing. Additional projects outside this agreement are quoted separately.


10. Termination & Exit. Either party may terminate with 30 days’ written notice. This period ensures adequate handover and management of resource allocation.


11. Governing Law. This agreement is governed by the laws of Victoria, Australia.


12. Electronic Execution. The parties agree that this Agreement may be executed electronically and that electronic signatures or click-wrap checkboxes carry the full weight of a handwritten signature under the Electronic Transactions Act 1999 (Cth) and the Electronic Transactions Act 2000 (Vic).
